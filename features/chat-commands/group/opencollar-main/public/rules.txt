Group Chat Rules
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
This chat is for OpenCollar related support and polite discussion.

Off-topic chat is welcome provided no one is actively seeking help for an OpenCollar issue.

Please follow these basic guidelines:

• Be respectful. No personal attacks, abuse or harassment.
• No spaming, advertising, or soliciting others.
• Do not use this chat to look for masters, subs, or hookups.
• No attacks on OpenCollar, its members, or its policies.
• Keep personal disputes out of group chat.
• Do not interrupt active help sessions with unrelated talk.
• Do not argue with the mods.

Violations may result in a warning, being muted, temp or perrma-banned from group chat at the sole discretion of the moderation team.
